About me

I am Eva, a professional travel photographer and content creator from Germany shooting aerial footage with my drone. I gave up my old job and life in Germany to travel full-time around Southeast Asia and Europe capturing the most beautiful sceneries. The endless possibilities of creating unique photos from an aerial perspective fascinate me.

My Values

I grew up to be conscious of the environment but traveling in the past years made me realise even more how crucial it is to protect our planet. I’ve seen places transform, forests disappear, rivers and oceans full of plastic. Ten years ago I went vegetarian followed by vegan a few years later which has been one of the best decisions of my life. Not only for my own health but especially for the positive effect it has on our planet.
