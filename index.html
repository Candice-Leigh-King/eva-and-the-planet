Hi!

I am Eva, a professional drone photographer from Germany. 

After working for years in finance and product management, I decided to leave my old job behind in the summer of 2021. I've been traveling full-time around Asia and Europe ever since, capturing the most beautiful scenery with my drone. 

My passion for travel and exploring our beautiful world evolved back in 2004. Living with a host family in the US for a year as a teenager was a life-changing experience.

I grew up to be conscious of the environment and live a sustainable life. Ten years ago I decided to stop eating meat, and three years later I transformed to living 100% plant-based. This has been one of the best decisions of my life. I’m fascinated by animals and how each creature has its unique capabilities as part of this incredible ecosystem we all live in. Choosing to eat plant-based gives animals a voice which they deserve. Not to mention the benefits for the environment and personal health. 

I fell in love with drone photography and instantly knew I wanted to combine this new passion with my love for nature. By showing the beautiful world we live in from above, I want to bring awareness to the importance of preserving it. My mission is to inspire others with my content to go out and see the world and its beauty to understand how important it is to protect it, so we all have something left to see and experience years ahead.
