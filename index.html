Welcome to my page!

My name is Eva, and I am a professional travel photographer and content creator currently living in Southeast Asia. 

My passion for drones was sparked during a trip to Thailand, where I instantly fell in love with aerial photography and videography. 

Seeing places from above for the first time that I’ve visited before was such a unique experience. The new perspectives and stunning aerial views I got were fascinating. Since then, I have been traveling full-time throughout Southeast Asia and Europe, capturing the most beautiful sceneries with my drone.

I have been living 100% plant-based for 8 years and it has been the best decision of my life. 

I grew up to be conscious of the environment but my travels in recent years made me realise even more how crucial it is to protect our planet. I’ve seen places transform, forests disappear, rivers and oceans full of plastic.

Ten years ago, I made the decision to stop eating meat, and two years later, I transitioned to a 100% plant-based lifestyle. This has been one of the best decisions of my life, as I am fascinated by animals and their unique role in this incredible ecosystem we all live in. Choosing to eat plant-based gives animals a voice which they rightfully deserve. Not to mention the positive impact it has on the planet and personal health.

My mission is to inspire others with my content, to explore and appreciate the beauty of our planet.

Flying my drone has been a passion of mine for quite some time now and I knew from the beginning I wanted to combine drone photography and videography with my love for nature and the environment.

Through drone photography and videography, I am able to showcase the stunning world we live in from a unique perspective, all while raising awareness about the importance of preserving our planet.

My mission is to inspire others with my content to explore and appreciate the beauty of our planet and understand the significance of protecting it. With every image and video I capture, I hope to encourage people to take action and make a difference, so that we can continue to enjoy the natural wonders of our world for years to come.

As a professional travel photographer and content creator, I am dedicated to creating breathtaking visuals that not only capture the beauty of a destination but also promote environmental responsibility and sustainability.

Lets have a chat and talk about how we can work together to create impactful content that inspires change.
